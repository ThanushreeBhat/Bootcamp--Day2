Top 5 freedom fighters of India

Top 5 freedom fighters of India

curated by Thanushree Bhat K G

1.Mahatma Gandhi

Known as the "Father of the Nation," Gandhi led the Indian independence movement through nonviolent resistance and civil disobedience. His philosophy of Satyagraha (truth force) inspired millions and ultimately contributed to India's freedom. Gandhi's approach was instrumental in securing India's independence.

2.Subhas Chandra Bose

A dynamic leader, Bose advocated for complete independence and formed the Indian National Army (INA) to fight against British rule. His slogan "Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom!" resonated with many and demonstrated his commitment to armed struggle.

3.Bhagat Singh

A revolutionary socialist, Bhagat Singh believed in armed revolution to overthrow British rule. He was involved in several acts of defiance, including the Central Legislative Assembly bombing, and became a symbol of resistance and patriotism for the youth.

4. Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

A key figure in the Indian National Congress, Patel played a crucial role in organizing the peasant movement and integrating the princely states into the Indian Union after independence. He was known as the "Iron Man of India" for his strong leadership

5. Jawaharlal Nehru

As the first Prime Minister of India, Nehru was a key architect of modern India. He was a strong advocate for democratic socialism and played a significant role in shaping India's foreign policy during the early years of independence.
